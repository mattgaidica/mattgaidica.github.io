About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Neuroscience at Washington University in St. Louis. I also serve as the Director of the Neurotech Hub, a new state-of-the-art shop that aims to accelerate ideation, prototyping, and bespoke applications for our research community.

In addition to that, I am an officer in the U.S. Air Force Reserve. Across the board, I leverage my background in engineering and science to address extraordinary challenges.
